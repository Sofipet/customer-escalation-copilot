# Release 2408 Quote Approval Updates

## Overview
Release 2408 prepared the workflow for a later approval service transition.

## What changed
The approval service now checks strategic renewal markers earlier in the request lifecycle.
This may affect which support troubleshooting path appears correct.

## Support impact
Support should prefer policy-based classification over older habit-based remediation.
No full restriction of manual override was introduced yet, but preconditions became stricter.

## Notes
Teams were advised to review legacy KB articles before the next major release.
