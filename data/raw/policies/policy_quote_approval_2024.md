# Quote Approval Policy 2024

## Purpose
This document defines quote approval rules for standard commercial deals, renewal deals, and support-managed exceptions for the 2024 operating model.

## Standard approval rules
Quotes with discounts below 10% may be approved automatically for standard deals when no exception flags are present.
Quotes with discounts from 10% to 20% require manager approval.
Quotes above 20% require director approval.
Support teams may review routing outcomes but should not change commercial thresholds.

## Manual override policy
Manual override may be used by support for selected approval failures when all of the following are true:
- the deal is a standard non-strategic deal,
- discount is below 20%,
- no pricing exception flag is active,
- support confirms that the failure is caused by known workflow latency.

Manual override must not be used to bypass missing approvals.

## Regional rules
Germany and other EU markets follow the same commercial thresholds, but local teams may use region-specific operational queues.

## Strategic renewals
Strategic renewal deals require finance review when annual contract value is above €100,000 or when discount exceeds 15%.

## Support guidance
If a customer reports an approval failure, support should verify the deal type, the applicable discount level, and the active workflow version before taking action.
