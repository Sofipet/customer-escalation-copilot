# Support Escalation Guidelines

## Purpose
This document defines how support should investigate and document quote approval escalations.

## Required information
Support should collect:
- customer region,
- deal type,
- annual contract value,
- discount level,
- current release version if known,
- exact error message or screenshot,
- whether the case is a renewal or strategic account.

## Resolution workflow
Support should search for the latest policy and release guidance before acting on older knowledge-base articles.
If the issue may be linked to a known incident, support should consult incident summaries and troubleshooting guidance.

## Handling conflicting guidance
If a support KB article conflicts with a current policy or release note, the support KB article should be treated as secondary.
Support should mention the conflict in the case notes and proceed with the current high-authority source.

## Output expectation
Every escalated case should end with a likely cause statement, a recommended next step, and cited evidence.
