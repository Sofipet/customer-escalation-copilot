# Strategic Renewal Exception Rules

## Purpose
This document clarifies handling of strategic renewals that require exception routing.

## Strategic renewal definition
A deal is treated as strategic if one or more of the following apply:
- annual contract value is above €100,000,
- the account is marked as protected,
- the renewal is linked to a critical retention risk,
- the commercial team requests special review.

## Override restrictions
Manual override is not permitted for strategic renewals when the workflow includes protected approval routing.
Support may collect evidence and route the case to Revenue Operations, but support must not bypass the workflow.

## Escalation process
If approval fails on a strategic renewal, support should:
1. confirm deal classification,
2. capture contract value and discount,
3. attach workflow error details,
4. route the case to Revenue Operations or Pricing Operations depending on the exception type.

## Note
Older support articles that mention manual override do not apply to strategic renewal cases under the 2025 exception model.
