# Quote Approval Policy 2025

## Purpose
This document describes the current quote approval rules for standard deals, strategic renewals, and discount exceptions under the 2025 policy baseline.

## Standard approval rules
Quotes with discounts below 8% may be approved automatically for standard deals when no exception conditions are triggered.
Quotes with discounts from 8% to 18% require manager approval.
Quotes above 18% require director approval.
For deals above €150,000 annual contract value, a finance checkpoint is required even if the discount would normally qualify for manager approval.

## Manual override policy
Manual override is permitted only for standard non-strategic deals with workflow failures caused by routing or timeout errors.
Manual override must not be used for strategic renewals, protected exception cases, or deals affected by release-restricted workflow behavior.
Support must always verify whether a release-specific restriction is active before using manual override.

## Regional rules
Germany and EU support flows must follow the most recent release behavior and current policy restrictions.
Where a release note explicitly narrows manual override behavior, the release note and current policy should be treated as authoritative.

## Strategic renewals
Strategic renewals require additional review when annual contract value exceeds €100,000, when discount exceeds 12%, or when a protected account flag is present.
Manual override is not allowed for strategic renewal deals in restricted workflow scenarios.

## Support guidance
When customers report approval failures, support should identify the deal type, contract value, discount level, region, and release context before recommending the next step.
If documents disagree, support should prefer the newest high-authority guidance.
