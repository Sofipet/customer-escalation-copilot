# Discount Threshold Policy

## Summary
This policy lists the default commercial approval thresholds used by the quote approval workflow.

## Thresholds
For standard deals:
- up to 8% discount: auto-approval when no exception flags are active
- above 8% and up to 18%: manager approval
- above 18%: director approval

For strategic renewals:
- up to 10% discount: manager approval when annual contract value is below €100,000
- above 10% or annual contract value above €100,000: director approval plus finance review

## Support implications
Support should not interpret a threshold as permission to use manual override.
Commercial thresholds and override permissions are separate controls.

## Notes
When troubleshooting escalation cases, support should combine this policy with the current quote approval policy and the latest release guidance.
