# Troubleshooting Approval Timeout

## Symptom
The customer submits a quote, but the approval step does not complete.

## Checks
Confirm whether the case is a standard deal, whether a known incident is active, and whether the request entered the expected regional queue.

## Resolution
If the issue is a true timeout on an otherwise eligible standard deal, support may follow current policy for manual remediation.
If the case is strategic, renewal-related, or region-restricted, do not attempt override and escalate according to policy.
