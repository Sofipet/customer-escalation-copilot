# Troubleshooting Quote Submission Blocked

## Symptom
The customer cannot complete quote submission because approval routing blocks the request.

## Checks
Confirm region, release version, queue path, and whether the account is part of a known incident.
For Germany and EU cases after release 2409, review incident notes and current policy.

## Guidance
If documentation conflicts, prefer the newer policy and release documentation over older support KB summaries.
