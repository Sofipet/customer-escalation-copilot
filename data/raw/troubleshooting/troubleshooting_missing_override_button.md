# Troubleshooting Missing Override Button

## Symptom
Support does not see the manual override control in the support tool.

## Most likely causes
- the case is no longer eligible under current policy,
- the deal is classified as strategic renewal,
- the region entered a protected path after release 2409,
- the support UI correctly reflects restricted behavior.

## Recommended response
Do not treat the missing button as a defect until deal classification and release context are confirmed.
Review the latest release notes and current policy before escalating.
