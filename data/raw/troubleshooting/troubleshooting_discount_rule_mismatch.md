# Troubleshooting Discount Rule Mismatch

## Symptom
Support believes the discount should qualify for approval, but the system routes the case differently.

## Common causes
- strategic renewal classification,
- annual contract value above the finance checkpoint,
- protected account flag,
- stale assumption that commercial threshold equals override eligibility.

## Guidance
Compare the discount threshold policy with strategic renewal rules and current release restrictions.
