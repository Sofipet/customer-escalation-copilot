# Incident Summary - Override Eligibility Confusion

## Summary
Several support escalations showed that agents were confusing commercial thresholds with override eligibility.

## Root causes
- stale KB usage,
- incomplete deal classification,
- missing awareness of strategic renewal restrictions,
- assumptions carried forward from 2024 policies.

## Recommendation
Support tools and AI workflows should explain why a case is non-overridable instead of only showing the threshold outcome.
