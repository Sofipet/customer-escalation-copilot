# Incident Summary - Germany Approval Failures

## Incident summary
After release 2409, Germany support reported a spike in escalations where approval could not be completed and manual override was not available.

## Findings
Many affected cases were strategic renewals or entered protected regional routing.
Support confusion was amplified by older KB articles that still described pre-2409 override behavior.

## Resolution guidance
For affected Germany cases, support should verify whether the case is strategic or protected before escalating to Revenue Operations.
Do not rely on legacy override assumptions.
