# Renewal Approval Support Steps

## Summary
This article covers support handling for renewal-related approval delays.

## Process
Confirm whether the case is a renewal, collect annual contract value, and check if retention-risk notes are present.
Support should avoid using standard-deal remediation until renewal classification is confirmed.

## Escalation note
Strategic renewals may require routing to Revenue Operations even if earlier cases were handled locally.
