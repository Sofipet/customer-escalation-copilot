# Legacy Guidance for Manual Override in Germany

## Summary
This article reflects older support guidance for Germany before later workflow restrictions were introduced.

## Typical support flow
If a quote approval failure occurs in Germany and the discount is below 20%, support may use manual override after confirming that no missing formal approval exists.
This workaround was commonly used for standard support-managed approval failures before the workflow tightened.

## Known exceptions
Do not use override for obvious policy violations or protected exception cases.
At the time of writing, most Germany support teams treated non-protected cases as eligible.

## Notes
This article predates later release changes and should be validated against current release notes and policy documents.
