# Manual Override Guidance for Germany

## Summary
This article describes support-managed manual override for approval failures in Germany under the pre-2409 workflow model.

## Typical support flow
If a customer reports that quote approval did not complete, support should confirm that the quote is a standard deal and that the discount is below 20%.
If those conditions are met and no known policy exception exists, support may use manual override to complete the case.
Germany support teams have used this flow to reduce queue delays for standard commercial deals.

## Known limitations
Do not use manual override when approvals are genuinely missing.
Do not use this article for protected exception cases.

## Notes
This article was written before later workflow restrictions and may require validation against newer release guidance.
