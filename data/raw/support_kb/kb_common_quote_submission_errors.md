# Common Quote Submission Errors

## Summary
This article lists frequent customer-facing errors during quote submission and approval.

## Common messages
- approval routing unavailable,
- renewal validation pending,
- override not available,
- regional queue mismatch,
- protected account review required.

## Support guidance
The phrase "override not available" should not automatically be treated as a UI bug.
It may reflect correct policy or release behavior.
