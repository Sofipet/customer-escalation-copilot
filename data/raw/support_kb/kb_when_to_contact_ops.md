# When Support Should Contact Operations

## Summary
Support should contact Operations when a quote approval issue cannot be resolved through standard troubleshooting or when policy prevents local remediation.

## Contact Operations if
- the deal is strategic,
- annual contract value is above €100,000,
- the workflow is release-restricted,
- the case is tied to a known incident,
- policy and KB guidance conflict.

## Why this matters
Escalating early reduces incorrect override attempts and helps preserve an audit trail.
