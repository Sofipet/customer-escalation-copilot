# Discount Exception Support Flow

## Summary
This article describes how support should document and route discount exception cases.

## Process
Support should verify discount level, contract value, deal type, and whether the customer is marked as strategic.
When thresholds are exceeded, support should not attempt to solve the issue locally through override.
Instead, support should capture the error details and escalate to the correct approval owner.

## Important distinction
Threshold eligibility and override eligibility are not the same.
A case with a moderate discount may still be non-overridable if it is strategic or release-restricted.
